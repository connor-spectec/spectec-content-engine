INPUTS:

- Paste the approved sequence plan from Step 02: [paste here]

TASK:
Create a research and evidence pack that makes the email content
credible, relevant, and safe to send.

RULES (follow all of these):
- Cross-reference the connected knowledge base documents first before
  flagging anything as missing information.
- Do not invent statistics, customer outcomes, or product capabilities.
- Only surface SpecTec-specific facts from uploaded documents.

OUTPUT — use this exact structure:
A) Key concepts that must be accurate (claims or product facts to verify)
B) Likely reader reactions and objections (per email type — prospecting / nurture / loyalty)
C) Claims-to-verify list — tag each item as:
  MUST VERIFY (cannot send without this)
  NICE TO HAVE (strengthens the email but not blocking)
D) Maritime-specific proof points or examples to include
E) Personalisation opportunities (fields or context that could be dynamically inserted)
F) What NOT to claim (risk list — things that could be wrong,
  create reputational issues, or feel presumptuous to a cold contact)
G) Missing information — what SpecTec needs to provide that is
  not in the uploaded documents (only flag after checking knowledge base)
